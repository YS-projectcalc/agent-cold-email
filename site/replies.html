The moment outreach becomes a conversation

A reply stops the sequence.

Coldrig records the response, prevents the scheduled follow-ups for that prospect, and makes the conversation visible to both the agent and the owner. The next message is an explicit action—not an unattended side effect.

Current transport

Reply events can be pushed to your own HTTPS endpoint (HMAC-signed) via configure_webhook, or polled through activity or the unified inbox — whichever fits your integration.

Reply lifecycle

Five visible state changes.

The service owns durable state; the agent owns interpretation and strategy. This separation keeps conversational judgment flexible while making suppression and sequence behavior deterministic.

Receive

The provider-facing activity path records the inbound message against its campaign and prospect.

Stop

Further scheduled sequence steps for that prospect are stopped at the service layer.

Surface

The reply appears in activity results and the tenant's unified inbox with its conversation context.

Decide

The agent can classify intent and propose an action; the owner can inspect what matters.

What the agent should do

Read context before replying.

  • Positive or curiousSummarize the signal, draft a relevant next step, and respect any owner approval rule.
  • Question or objectionAnswer only from known product context; escalate unsupported claims.
  • Not interestedRecord the outcome and do not restart outreach.
  • Unsubscribe, complaint, or sensitive responseSuppress immediately and avoid persuasive follow-up.
