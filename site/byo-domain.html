Bring the domain. Keep the risk visible.

Coldrig's BYO (bring-your-own) domain intake is live in the API and MCP surface — you can register a domain, run the pre-flight scan, and track consent today. Real DNS delegation, live reputation checks, and platform-provisioned mailboxes on your own domain activate through the same short concierge step as real sending generally; registering a domain does not itself provision anything real until your account is activated.

Recommended default

Use dedicated adjacent domains or a delegated sending subdomain. Do not casually place cold outreach on the primary domain that carries your website, employee email, and customer trust.

Domain choices

Not all “bring your own” paths are equal.

Domain reputation has consequences outside a campaign. Coldrig's intended production flow makes that tradeoff explicit before any DNS or sender action.

PREFERRED

Adjacent domains

Dedicated, brand-relevant domains isolate outreach reputation from the primary brand domain while remaining recognizable to recipients.

CONSIDER

Delegated subdomain

A customer-controlled sending subdomain can simplify ownership and DNS boundaries, but it remains connected to the parent brand.

HIGH RISK

Primary domain

Using the main website and employee-email domain concentrates reputation risk. It should require explicit, informed owner consent—not agent inference.

Before activation

The owner should be able to answer these.

  • Who owns and can edit DNS?The account must not depend on credentials the owner cannot recover.
  • What business traffic shares this reputation?Website, transactional, support, and employee mail change the risk calculation.
  • What happens when the service ends?DNS ownership stays with the customer; mailbox and vendor portability must be confirmed before activation.
  • Has the primary-domain risk been accepted explicitly?Silence, a prompt, or an agent default is not informed consent.
Honest implementation status

Live intake, concierge-activated provisioning.

Domain registration, the pre-flight abuse/reputation scan, and consent tracking are live, reachable tools in the API and MCP surface today (get_byo_domains, configure_byo_domain) — this is not a design document. What activates through the same concierge step as real sending generally: actual DNS delegation, live reputation/blocklist checks, and platform-provisioned mailboxes on your own domain. A burned BYO domain hard-pauses and is never auto-replaced, unlike a burned lookalike domain, because it's your business's domain, not a disposable one. There is no published pricing yet for BYO-connected mailboxes specifically. Use the sandbox to validate workflow behavior, or request activation to start a real intake.
