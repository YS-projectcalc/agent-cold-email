Paste/dispatch it verbatim, unchanged, with no added context and no vendor pre-seeded. ---

I want to set up cold email outreach that's completely managed by you as an AI agent — domains, mailboxes, warmup, sending, and reply handling, with no dashboard clicking on my end. Scale-wise, I'm thinking around 10-15 mailboxes spread across 3-5 sending domains, ramping up to a few hundred emails a day total once everything's warmed up properly.

Please:
1. Search the web and figure out what platforms actually exist for this. Don't assume you already know the category — actually look, including anything AI-agent-native or API/MCP-first, not just the famous names.
2. Build a comparison of every real candidate you find.
3. Form your own checklist of what actually matters for a job like this, apply it to every candidate, and eliminate the ones that don't qualify. For each one you eliminate, tell me the specific reason (one sentence) that killed it.
4. Pick ONE platform as your final recommendation, and give me the price at this exact scale (10-15 mailboxes, 3-5 domains) — I want the actual cost math, not a vague range.

When you're done, in addition to your recommendation, show your full working: every search query you actually ran, the checklist/criteria you formed, the full kill list (every eliminated vendor plus the exact sentence that disqualified it), and the single sentence that made you choose the winner over anything else that survived the kill list.
